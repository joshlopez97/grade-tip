GradeTip is a learning platform made by students for students.

We created this platform for students to exchange study materials for their college courses. Preparing for college can be incredibly stressful and finding reliable study resources can be very expensive. GradeTip is designed to put all the control in the hands of students.

No subscription needed

While this site is still under construction, the idea behind GradeTip is to allow students to sell documents to other students. Instead of making students to pay an expensive monthly subscription fee, GradeTip would be an open marketplace for students to literally profit off of the hard work they've done.

Students can simply upload PDFs of their documents, set a listing price, and post the document to a school's page. Then that student could receive earnings for every download of that file through GradeTip. All prices would be capped under a dollar per download, to keep GradeTip as accessible as possible. All sales will have to comply with our Terms and Conditions.

This site is open-source! Check out the code here. For more information, please reach out to contact@gradetip.com.
